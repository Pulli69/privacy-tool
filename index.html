What is Seismic?

Privacy Blockchain, Explained Simply

Think of Seismic like Ethereum, but with privacy. It's a blockchain where everything stays private by default.

On a regular blockchain, everyone sees every wallet balance, every trade, every payment. It's like living in a financial fishbowl.

Seismic changes that. You can still do everything (payments, loans, swaps), but your activity stays hidden.

For developers: Write normal Solidity code. Build just like Ethereum. Get privacy for free.

For regulators: They still see what they need. Compliance tools are built in. Privacy and regulation work together.

Hidden Transactions

Your balances, trades, and history stay private from everyone except you.

Compliance Works

Regulators see what they need. Built-in screening and reporting tools.

Solidity Works

Write code exactly like Ethereum. No new language. No new learning.

Real Financial Apps

Payments, loans, trading, swaps. Real products that protect privacy.
